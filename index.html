Our Mission

Save the trees.

Over 27 Million business cards are printed daily. Thats 10 Billion annually. 15,000,000 kg of paper are used per year for business cards alone. Approx 41,000 trees are cut down just to make business cards. With Bridge Card, we won’t have the need to cut down 1 tree.

Save the fees.

Never will you need to buy a box of 500 business cards again, just to switch positions and have 300 leftover, useless business cards. With Bridge Card, you will only ever need…. Just one.

Network with ease.

88% of business cards are thrown away within the first week of receiving them (thats 8.8 billion physical cards gone to waste in the first week alone…). The #1 reason is because the receiver doesn’t need the business or service right now. WIth Bridge Card, have the person’s info stored in your contacts and easily accesible whenever you need them
